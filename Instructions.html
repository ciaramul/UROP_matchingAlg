Scenario

You are in a group of {{Constants.players_per_group}} people. Each of the participants in your group will be matched with one of {{Constants.num_sm}} slot machines that will deliver a payout of between {{0|c}} and {{ Constants.payout_max|c }}. Each round you can select to return to the same slot machine, switch slot machines, or quit the game entirely.

On the selection page, you will have {{ Constants.timeout_seconds }} seconds to make your decision. If you allow the game to timeout, you will, by default, quit the game.

This game will be played for {{Constants.num_rounds}} rounds.

Returning to the Same Slot Machine

The same slot machine will fluctuate in payout slightly between rounds, but will return a constant average payout while you are accessing it.

Switching Slot Machines

If you choose to switch slot machines, you will be matched with one of the {{Constants.num_sm}} slot machines in your group that you have not accessed previously and that is not occupied by another member of your group.

Quitting the Game

If you elect to quit the game or allow the game to timeout, you will receive a constant amount added to your total payout, but will not participate in subsequent rounds.

If you choose to quit the first round, you will receive {{Constants.init_quit_pay|c }}. For every round you play, this compensation upon quitting will decrease by {{Constants.dec_quit_pay|c}}.
